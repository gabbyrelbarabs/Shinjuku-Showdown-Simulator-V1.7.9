Basic Info:

Welcome to Shinjuku Showdown Simulator Alpha 1.7.9!

This game replicates the greatest battle in Jujutsu History; The Strongest Sorcerer of Today versus The Strongest Sorcerer in History!

The battle takes place in Shinjuku, Tokyo, Japan. Which has been specially evacuated for this exorcism to take place.

Strategize, utilize the battlefield, or brute force your way as you try to defeat the King of Curses, Ryomen Sukuna, as fast as you can.

You may find mysterious, special objects in the battlefield that could possibly help you in your battle:

  • Red: Strengthens you up, doubling your overall damage.
  • Orange: Powers you up, allowing you to use one of your abilities immediately.
  • Blue: Activates your Infinity for 5 seconds.
  • Green: Automatically activates your RCT with no cost.
  • Brown: Increases your defenses and reflects damage back towards the boss for 10 seconds.
  • Yellow: Boosts your speed and agility, increasing your dodging skills!

Controls:

  • Computer: Use ASWD or the arrow keys to move around. Double tap A or D to dash, other controls will be mentioned in Player Info.
  • Mobile: Nonexistent. (for now)

Good luck, and may the greatest sorcerer win!

Player Info:

Name: Satoru Gojo

Age: 28

Height: 6'3" or 190cm

Weight: 176lbs or 80kg

Abilities:

  • Dash: Dash around and through attacks to dodge. Double tap the movement buttons to Dash.
  • Close Combat: Your main offense when in close combat with the target.
  • Black Flash: A rare occurence when the user lands an attack, applying Cursed Energy within one one-millionth of a second, dealing bonus damage.
  • Lapse Blue: Your main ranged attack, dealing around 75 damage, it pulls targets towards you if hit, allowing you to create combos. Left click to use.
  • Reversal Red: Your main special attack, dealing around 200-450 damage, charge it up with 5 Lapse Blues to use. It pushes targets away from you if hit, best used if the enemy gets close. Right click to use.
  • Max Blue: An amplified Lapse Blue that can be used if charged up with 150 Lapse Blues. Dealing around 1000-2500 damage, targets hit will be pulled right onto the user, to be then combo'd with a Black Flash. Combos well with Max Red. Press B to use.
  • Max Red: An amplified Reversal Red that can be used if charged up with 30 Reversal Reds. Dealing around 2500-6000 damage, targets hit will be launched away. Combos well with Max Blue. Press R to use.
  • Hollow Purple: A powerful attack that can be used if charged up with 100 Lapse Blues and 20 Reversal Reds. It deals around 3000-7500 damage. Press C to use.
  • Transmission: A unique form of Reversal Red that can be used if charged up with 150 blues, allowing you to teleport wherever you wish at an extra cost of a Dash. Press T to use.
  • Six Eyes Awakening: Go beyond your limits and awaken your true potential by unlocking the full power of your Six Eyes. Charged up with 1000 blues and 100 reds, activating this will increase Gojo's damage, speed, and even allow Gojo to use abilities way faster than normal, and use Infinity on command, all in exchange for Gojo's mentality. Press Y to use.
  • Domain Expansion: Unlimited Void: Gojo's most powerful technique that can be charged up if your technique has been used 1500 times. It increases Gojo's stats greatly and stuns all targets hit for 10 seconds. Press V to use.
  • Reverse Cursed Technique: A unique ability using Positive Energy that can be charged up if your technique has been used 300 times. It recovers Gojo's technique and stamina, healing you by around 20-30% over the course of 5 seconds. Press X to use.

Use his abilites wisely, as his strongest attacks cannot be used all the time.

Boss Info:

The King of Curses himself, Ryomen Sukuna.

A violent, merciless force of nature, killing anyone in his way.

Age: 1000+ (Born from the Heian-Era)

Height: Unknown

Weight: Unknown

Abilities:

  • Close Combat: His main offense when in close combat with enemies.
  • Cleave: A unique attack he could land on targets, breaking defenses and dealing extra damage.
  • Dismantle: His main attack, a ranged-type attack that slashes foes from afar.
  • Slicerweb: A special attack he uses, smashing into the ground and slashing everything that stays on the ground(platforms included), stunning them.
  • Cleave Rush: A special attack he uses, charging at his target and obliterating them with a powerful cleave. But it stuns him as well from the recoil.
  • Reverse Cursed Technique: The boss may also use this unique technique, replenishing his technique and stamina, regenerating his HP by around 20-30% over the course of 5 seconds.

------------------------------------------Phase 2------------------------------------------

However, Sukuna may get desperate, quickly using RCT while summoning some new foes:

  • Eight-handled Sword Divergent Sila Divine General Mahoraga: A powerful entity summoned from his vessel, Megumi Fushiguro,'s Ten Shadows Technique. With a huge amount of durability, this guy will defend Sukuna with all his life. He also has a unique ability to adapt, getting even stronger and more durable over time, so make sure to eliminate him before he becomes invincible.
  • Chimera Beast Agito: Another powerful entity created from several other creatures from the Ten Shadows technique. With her extremely fast speed and a powerful offense, but horrible defenses, she will stay in the high grounds, guarding the target from taking advantage of the high ground and stealing the mysterious objects. Make sure to neutralize her if you want your precious power-ups.

    • NOTE: Sukuna is at his toughest in this phase, so be careful, and find a way to exterminate him immediately.

    ------------------------------------------Phase 3------------------------------------------

    However, Sukuna may get desperate once more, as he may then finally use his most powerful technique...

    Domain Expansion: Malevolent Shrine

  • When this ability activates, Sukuna will enter his original, most powerful form, replenishing all of his stamina and greatly increasing all of his stats, and powering up his abilities. Targets within the domain will also begin getting slashed and torn apart by a barrage of Cleaves and Dismantles as time goes on, so be careful.

    • When Sukuna enters his final form, he will also reawaken hidden techniques he's kept a secret from his time in the Heian-Era:

  • Kamutoke: A powerful technique Sukuna can conjure up, striking the battlefield with lightning, shocking and stunning anything hit.
  • Fugue(Open): Sukuna's most powerful technique. When fired, it will burn up the entire battlefield if it hits something, ensuring death to everything. So be careful if he begins to chant this.
